Job Purpose:

To drive our company's financial strategy, and provide valuable insights for decision-making. The responsibilities will include conducting thorough financial analysis and collaborating with cross-functional teams to develop financial models.

Job Description:

  • Oversees projects that relate directly to an organization's revenue and expenses, cash flow, long-term investments, and reporting
  • Prepares cost-benefit analyses for projects across departments while identifying areas where revenue, profitability, and cash flow can be improved
  • Skilled in budgeting and financial management, both to help the teams complete projects according to estimated budgets and to oversee an organization's financial performance
  • Balances big-picture business goals and long-term forecasting with day-to-day oversight of revenue, expenditures, and cash flow in order to make well-informed decisions about emerging opportunities and areas for improvement
  • Builds and works with cross-functional teams. The ability to coordinate across departments and motivate staff members to complete projects according to both internal and external deadlines is vital
  • Actively seeks methods to enhance overall efficiency and performance. This can mean taking a more active role in the day-to-day work within the project lifecycle or identifying cost-saving opportunities at the organizational level
  • Identifies areas for improvement and develops solutions to prevent project delays and help a company meet its budgetary and cash flow goals
  • Frequently communicates with both team members and executives during the project lifecycle. Frequently needs to present findings to stakeholders and effectively communicate with team members and departments
  • Communicates goals and expectations to team members, shares progress and asks questions of organization leaders
  • Develops, presents, and oversees financial budgets on quarterly and annual basis in addition to 5-year plans
  • Collaborates with executives, project managers, and cross-departmental leadership to set budget goals and ensure they’re followed, including tracking results and comparing them to key financial targets

Requirements:

1- Required education and experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance or Accounting, preferably in Finance
  • Basic knowledge and understanding of financial planning and analysis concepts
  • CMA, MBA, CPA would be an advantage

2- Required languages and computer skills:

  • Excellent verbal & written English and Arabic
  • Good knowledge of working with budget models
  • Experience with ERP systems, preferably Oracle Fusion
  • Knowledge of Financial information management modules is a plus (ex: Power BI, Enterprise Planning Module, etc.)
